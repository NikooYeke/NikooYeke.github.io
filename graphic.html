Graphic Design
Catalogue Cover and DVD Design "Golizeh Co"

Golizheh is a print company with focus on special prints such as dry offset and heliograph. So I decided to use textures, colors, and shapes for their catalog and CD package design which evokes the ink in the viewer's mind.

The poster of Shiraz Novel Award

When my manager told me the need a poster for Shiraz Novel Award, I was driving to the office. The first thing that came to my mind was the use of a bird feather because writers used it in the past to write, and this could be a good symbol for that event. I also used yellow to show that authors can illuminate the course of human life by writing books. Fortunately, this project was noticed by my manager and the participants in that event.
